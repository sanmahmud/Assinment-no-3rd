The first interior images of the Tonino Lamborghini project in Dubai have been revealed, showcasing sleek, contemporary style that will include luxury design products set to give the residential development a distinctive Italian touch. Working in collaboration with Chinese real estate developer, Oriental Pearls, the multi-million dollar Tonino Lamborghini Residences is currently taking shape in Dubai's Meydan One district, and aims to bring the passion and spirit of Italy to the global market with distinctive products, inspired by Italian industrial design and the Lamborghini family mechanical heritage.
